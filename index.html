How It Works

Your Path to Halal Certification

Our certification process is thorough, transparent, and internationally recognised. Designed to give your business a halal certificate that opens doors to global Muslim markets.

1
Getting Started

Submit Your Application

Begin by completing and submitting your halal certification application to DHCE. Provide details about your business, product range, ingredients, and production processes so our team can assess your requirements.

2
Quotation

Receive Your Certification Quote

Following a review of your application, DHCE will issue a tailored quote for your certification. This covers the full scope of the audit and certification process specific to your business and product categories.

3
Agreements

Accept Quote & Sign Agreements

Once you accept the quote, DHCE will issue a Halal Certification Agreement and a Confidentiality Agreement. These set out the terms of certification and protect your business information throughout the process.

4
Audit Scheduling

Schedule Your On-Site Halal Audit

DHCE will work with you to schedule a convenient date for your on-site audit. Two qualified DHCE auditors will be assigned to conduct a thorough inspection of your facilities, production processes, ingredients, and supply chain.

5
Audit

On-Site Audit & Findings Report

Two DHCE auditors conduct the on-site halal audit at your premises. Following the audit, a detailed Halal Audit Report is issued outlining any findings. Your business will be required to address and close all findings before proceeding.

6
Review

DHCE Decision Committee Review

With all audit findings closed, your full application is reviewed by the DHCE internal Decision Committee. The committee rigorously assesses the audit report, findings closure, and your overall compliance before making a certification decision.

7
Certified

Halal Certificate Issued & Access Global Markets

Upon approval by the Decision Committee, your official DHCE Halal Certificate is issued. Globally recognised and accepted by major halal authorities worldwide, your certificate opens your business to Muslim consumers across international markets.

Why Choose DHCE

Certification That Opens Global Markets

As Ireland's only 100% Irish-owned, accredited halal certification body, DHCE gives your business the credibility and reach to access the world's 1.8 billion Muslim consumers.

Globally Recognised Certification

DHCE certificates are recognised by leading international halal authorities including JAKIM (Malaysia), MUIS (Singapore), and EIAC, giving your products access to some of the world's most demanding halal markets.

Ireland's Only Accredited Body

We are the only 100% Irish-owned, fully accredited halal certification authority in Ireland. Our independence and local expertise mean faster service, clearer communication, and genuine accountability.

Rigorous Sharia-Compliant Standards

Our certification process follows strict Islamic dietary law across every stage from ingredients, production, storage to supply chain ensuring your halal claim is authentic, verifiable, and defensible.

Food, Cosmetics & Pharmaceuticals

DHCE certifies across multiple product categories including food and beverages, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical products, making us a single trusted partner as your halal product range grows.

Grow Your Business & Export Reach

Halal certification is increasingly required by importers, retailers, and distributors in Muslim-majority markets. DHCE certification signals quality and trust, helping you win contracts and expand internationally.

End-to-End Expert Support

From your initial application through to audit preparation and ongoing compliance, our team provides hands-on guidance at every step so you're never navigating the process alone.
