Halal Certification Schemes

Whether you produce food, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, or run a logistics operation, DHCE has an internationally recognised halal certification scheme designed for your business.

What We Certify

Choose the Right Scheme for Your Business

DHCE offers halal certification across six product and service categories. Each scheme is designed to meet the specific compliance requirements of that sector — ensuring your certificate is credible, defensible, and globally recognised.

Food Products

Covers all food and beverage manufacturing, including ingredients, processing, packaging, and contamination controls. Applies to FMCG manufacturers, food processors, ingredient suppliers, abattoirs, and dairy producers.

Unlock access to Muslim-majority retail markets across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and beyond.

Cosmetics

Certifies skincare, makeup, haircare, and personal care products. Verifies that raw materials, formulations, and production processes are free from prohibited animal derivatives and non-compliant alcohol-based ingredients.

Position your brand in the fast-growing global halal beauty market, valued at over $100 billion.

Pharmaceuticals

Covers medicinal products, supplements, vaccines, OTC products, and pharmaceutical ingredients. Verifies gelatin sources, production line integrity, and contamination controls against halal standards.

Meet the growing demand from Muslim patients and healthcare providers who require halal-verified medicines.

Non-Food Items

Applies to materials used in halal production lines, including lubricants, filters, cleaning agents, packaging, and processing aids. This certification ensures these inputs do not compromise the halal integrity of the final product.

Become a preferred supplier to halal-certified food and pharma manufacturers who require compliant inputs.

Animal Feed

Certifies feed products, supplements, and additives used in halal livestock and poultry farming. Ensures the entire halal husbandry chain remains compliant, from feed through to slaughter and processing.

Support the integrity of the halal supply chain and become a trusted supplier to halal meat producers.

Services

Covers logistics, transport, storage, catering, cleaning, and Islamic financial services. Certifies that service operations are conducted in accordance with halal principles from end to end.

Differentiate your service business and become a compliant partner within certified halal supply chains.
Scope Codes

Full Scopes of Certification

DHCE's certification scope follows internationally recognised classification codes. These codes define the precise category of activity your business operates in and will appear on your official DHCE halal certificate.

Code Category Examples of Sectors
A Farming 1 — Animals Fish farming; egg production; milk production; beekeeping; fishing; hunting
B Farming 2 — Plants Fruits; vegetables; cereals; spices; horticultural products
C Processing 1 — Perishable Animal Products Animal slaughtering; poultry processing; eggs; dairy; fish products
D Processing 2 — Perishable Vegetable Products Fresh juices; preserved fruits; fresh and preserved vegetables
E Processing 3 — Long Shelf Life Products Canned products; biscuits; snacks; beverages; pasta; flour; sugar; salt
F Feed Production Feed products; supplements and additives for livestock and poultry
G Food Service Hotels; restaurants; catering operations
H Distribution Retail outlets; shops; wholesalers; importers and distributors
I Services Water supply; cleaning; waste disposal; product development; veterinary services; Islamic financial services
J Transport & Storage Logistics; cold chain transport; warehousing and storage facilities
K Equipment Manufacturing Industrial food processing equipment; vending machines
L Chemical & Biochemical Manufacturing Food additives; dietary supplements; oils; cleaning agents; processing aids; microorganisms
M Packaging & Wrapping Materials Food-contact packaging; wrapping materials for halal products
N Other Materials Manufacturing Cosmetics; textiles; leather products and other non-food manufactured goods
